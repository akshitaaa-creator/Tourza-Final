6 DAYS

Serene Kashmir Valley

Experience the "Paradise on Earth." From the floating gardens of Dal Lake to the snow-capped peaks of Gulmarg, this journey is crafted for those seeking nature's finest views at an unbeatable value.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & romantic sunset Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
Day 2: Srinagar Local: Visit Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Shankaracharya Temple.
Day 3: Day trip to Gulmarg: Gondola ride (Phase 1) and meadow exploration.
Day 4: Transfer to Pahalgam: En route visit Saffron fields and Awantipora ruins.
Day 5: Pahalgam exploration: Visit Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari.
Day 6: Morning breakfast at the hotel and departure from Srinagar Airport.

Inclusions

  • • 4 Nights in comfortable 3-star hotels + 1 Night in a Deluxe Houseboat.
  • • Daily breakfast and dinner (MAP Plan) included.
  • • 1-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
  • • Dedicated private AC vehicle (Non-AC in hills) for all transfers.
  • • All toll taxes, parking, and driver allowances.
